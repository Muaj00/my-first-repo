From Panchagarh To Kanchenjunga

Panchagarh is the northenmost district of Bangladesh from which kanchenjunga can be seen, surrounded on all three sides by a 27 Km boundary wall of India. Usually the cloudless sky of winter the peaks of the snow-white mountains glisten in the sun and just then it is possible to enjoy the alluring beauty of kanchenjunga. Jaflong Sylhet

Jaflong is a hill station and tourist destination in the Division of Sylhet, Bangladesh. It is located in Gowainghat Upazila of Sylhet District and situated at the border between Bangladesh and the Indian state of Meghalaya, overshadowed by subtropical mountains and rainforests.
